ABOUT ME

I am researching using convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs) for real-time object tracking in video data. In my research, I am developing fast and robust algorithms with the eventual goal of fully tracking laboratory procedures to reduce errors in experiments and increase reproducibility.
